BSc.It in Software Development Course Details

BSc (Bachelor of Science) in IT is basically about storing, processing, securing, and managing information. This degree is primarily focused on subjects such as software, databases, and networking. The BSc IT course is awarded for completing a programme of study in the field of software development, software testing, software engineering, web design, databases, programming, computer networking and computer systems. BSc IT course Graduates with an information technology background are able to perform technology tasks relating to the processing, storing, and communication of information between computers, mobile phones, and other electronic devices. BSc IT course is essential for candidates who wish to enter into the field of programming, network or database management. Those who are passionate about handling computer systems can choose this course. Candidates who are interested in pursuing a BSc IT and Software can check all the relevant details below.

Bsc IT Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must have passed their 10+2 level of education from a recognised educational Board. They must have Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as the main subjects, and score a minimum of 50% marks.
